AI

The SSI resource limit is $2,000 for an individual. According to SSA guidance, countable resources generally include cash, checking and savings accounts, stocks, and bonds. Some things are typically excluded — your primary home, one vehicle, household goods, and certain trust accounts like ABLE accounts (up to annual limits).

Since this is a general rule and your situation may have unique factors — for example, joint accounts, recent gifts, or trust arrangements — I'd recommend confirming with your local SSA office or a benefits counselor before making any moves with your accounts.
