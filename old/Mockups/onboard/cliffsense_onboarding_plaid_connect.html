Step 1 of 3 · Connect your bank

Connect your bank securely

We use Plaid — the same service trusted by Venmo, Robinhood, and most major banking apps — to read your transaction history. Your login credentials are never shared with CliffSense.

Plaid
CliffSense uses Plaid to connect to your bank
Plaid handles the actual login. We only see the read-only data we need to monitor your thresholds — and you can disconnect anytime in one click.
What we see
  • Account balances
  • Transaction history (last 24 months)
  • Account name and last 4 digits
What we never see
  • Your bank password or login
  • Your full account number
  • Ability to move or withdraw money
