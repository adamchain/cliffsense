4. Description of Services
4.1 Platform Overview
The TrustEase Platform is an AI-powered benefits protection and compliance monitoring tool designed to assist individuals with disabilities and their authorized representatives. Services include:
Benefits Compliance Monitoring: Real-time tracking of income, assets, and financial transactions against program-specific eligibility thresholds for SSI, SSDI, DAC, Medicare, Medicaid, SNAP, QMB, and other programs.
Bank Account Integration: Connection to financial institutions through Plaid for automated balance monitoring, transaction categorization, and financial data synchronization.
AI-Powered Benefits Advisor: An artificial intelligence chatbot providing personalized guidance on benefits compliance, program rules, reporting obligations, and risk mitigation strategies.
Alert System: Automated notifications when financial activity approaches or exceeds program thresholds, when reporting deadlines are imminent, or when regulatory changes may affect benefits.
Document Management: Upload, storage, and AI-powered text extraction of benefits-related documents.
Compliance Dashboard: Visual dashboards displaying risk indicators for each enrolled program, historical balance tracking, income analysis, and compliance status summaries.
Benefits News: Aggregation of regulatory updates, policy changes, and news relevant to government benefits programs.
